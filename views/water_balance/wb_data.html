Model Description
Thornthwaite - Mather Water Balance (no snow)

if Pn > Epn

  • Sn = min {Sn-1 + Pn – Epn, K}
  • Qn = max {Sn-1 +Pn –Epn –K, 0}
if Pn <= Epn
  • Sn = Sn-1 * exp(Pn –Epn /K)
  • Qn = 0
En = Pn – ΔSn –Qn

where P is the precipitation (mm), Ep is the potential evapotranspiration (mm), S is the soil water storage (mm), Εn is the actual evapotranspiration (mm), Q is the surplus of water (mm), Qn is the surface water flow (mm).

The soil moisture capacity K corresponds to the maximum capacity of retaining soil moisture and is expressed as follows (SCS, 1972):
K= 25.4 * [(1000/Cn) - 10]
